Comprehensive Mold Inspection Services in Tampa Bay

Professional mold detection, testing, and assessment for residential and commercial properties

Visual Mold Inspection

Our certified inspectors conduct thorough visual examinations of your property, identifying visible mold growth, water stains, and moisture intrusion points throughout your home or business.

Air Quality Testing

We use specialized air sampling pumps to measure mold spore concentrations in your indoor air. Samples are sent to AIHA-accredited labs for precise analysis and comparison to outdoor control samples.

Surface Sampling

Using tape lifts and swab samples, we collect material from suspected mold colonies for laboratory identification. This determines the exact mold species present and potential health risks.

Moisture Mapping & IR Scanning

Our thermal imaging cameras detect hidden moisture behind walls, under floors, and above ceilings—identifying problem areas before mold can grow. We create detailed moisture maps of your property.

Pre-Purchase Mold Inspection

Buying a home in Tampa? Don't skip the mold inspection. We identify existing issues and potential problems before you close, giving you negotiation power and peace of mind about your investment.

Post-Remediation Verification

After mold removal, we perform clearance testing to confirm the remediation was successful and spore levels return to normal. Essential for insurance claims and property transactions.
